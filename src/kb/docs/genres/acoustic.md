Acoustic recordings centre on unamplified or lightly amplified instruments — fingerpicked steel-string guitar, upright piano, voice, and small ensembles — captured with minimal processing. Tempos generally sit between 60 and 100 BPM, supporting a contemplative, conversational pace. Acousticness scores are uniformly very high — typically above 0.85 — because the spectral content is dominated by wood, strings, and vocal harmonics rather than synthesised sources. The genre fits naturally with relaxed, melancholy, and happy moods, and is a frequent choice for intimate, lyric-driven storytelling.
