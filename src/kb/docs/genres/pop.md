Pop tracks lean on tight verse-chorus structures, hook-forward melodies, and dense, polished production. Tempos most commonly sit between 100 and 132 BPM, with energy and danceability values typically above 0.7. The texture is heavily electronic — programmed drums, synth pads, layered vocals — so acoustic content is usually under 0.3. Pop pairs naturally with happy and upbeat moods, and can cross into intense territory when the production turns toward arena-scale drums and stacked synths.
