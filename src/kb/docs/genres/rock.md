Rock is built around amplified electric guitars, prominent drum kits, and bass-driven low end. Tempos span a wide band — from mid-90s on slower, brooding cuts to over 150 BPM on driving anthems — but energy values almost always run above 0.6. Acoustic content stays low because distortion, drum overheads, and electric bass dominate the spectrum. The genre's emotional range is broad: it pairs with intense and moody moods most often, and can support focused listening when the arrangement is steady and groove-led rather than chaotic.
