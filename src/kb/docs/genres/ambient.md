Ambient prioritises atmosphere over rhythm — long, evolving textures, sustained synth pads, and processed field recordings rather than verse-chorus structure. Tempos are slow when present at all, generally between 50 and 75 BPM, and energy values rarely exceed 0.4. The genre's high acousticness number is misleading: it scores high because the audio lacks the percussive transients that would otherwise pull it down, not because the instrumentation is acoustic in origin. Ambient is the natural backdrop for relaxed, chill, and focused moods, and is often used as a "background music for thinking" listening context.
