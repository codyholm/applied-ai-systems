Broken Neon runs at 112 BPM but feels slower because the drums sit deep in the mix under a long, plate-reverbed pad. Valence reads under 0.45, pulling the affect toward washed-out melancholy without letting energy drop too far. It's a cut for after-midnight listening — empty streets, blurred storefront signs, the city quieter than it should be.
