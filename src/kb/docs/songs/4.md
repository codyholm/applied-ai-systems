Library Rain pairs a 72 BPM kick-and-snare pattern with a soft piano motif and audible rain field-recording in the background. Acousticness is unusually high for the genre — the pads and piano take more space than the drums in the mix. It's a study-session standby: long-form, low-event, designed to recede behind the page.
