Gym Hero hammers a 132 BPM four-on-the-floor with sidechained synths and a stacked-vocal hook that lands hard on every chorus. Energy is at the catalog ceiling and acousticness drops to almost nothing — the mix is densely electronic from kick to vocal. It's built for workouts: clear tempo grid, big release moments, and no quiet spaces to lose momentum.
