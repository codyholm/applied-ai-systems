Night Drive Loop pulls the synthwave palette toward its moodier end — a 110 BPM arpeggiated bass synth, gated reverb on the drums, and a saw-wave lead that hangs in the high mids. Valence stays under 0.5, giving the track a brooding cast even at moderate energy. It's the canonical night-driving cut: long stretches of road, dim dashboard light, no need to talk.
