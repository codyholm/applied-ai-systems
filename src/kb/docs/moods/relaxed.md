Relaxed is a calmer cousin of chill — even lower energy, with valence that stays mildly positive rather than neutral. Energy values typically sit between 0.15 and 0.45, and valence between 0.45 and 0.80. Common genre pairings are acoustic, jazz, and ambient — styles where instrumentation is sparse, tempos are unhurried, and the recording captures small-room intimacy rather than dense production. Relaxed tracks fit dinner conversations, slow Sunday mornings, and any context where the music is supposed to recede into the background and stay there.
