Happy is the canonical bright-affect mood: high valence pushes the recommendation toward uplifting harmonic content — major keys, rising melodic contours, and bright timbres. Energy values tend to sit between 0.55 and 0.95 because joyful tracks usually move forward with momentum, even on mid-tempo songs. The mood pairs most commonly with pop, indie pop, and acoustic — genres that lean on hook-forward songwriting and warm timbres. Listeners reach for happy tracks during morning routines, daylight commutes, and social gatherings where the goal is to keep the energy lifted.
