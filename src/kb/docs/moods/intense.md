name: intense
energy_range: [0.75, 0.99]
valence_range: [0.30, 0.80]

Intense tracks push energy values above 0.75 and emphasise driving rhythm, dense production, and prominent low end. Valence is wide-band — intense music can be triumphant and high-valence (think arena pop) or dark and lower-valence (think hard rock or aggressive trap) — but the floor for energy stays high. Common genre pairings are rock, pop, hip hop, and synthwave on its faster cuts. Listeners reach for intense tracks during workouts, focused-output bursts, and anywhere a clear adrenaline floor is helpful.
